About Us

What we have done

At LiveStrong Fitness, we’re proud to have made a lasting impact on our members and the local community. Over the years, we’ve:

Empowered over 6,000 members to take control of their health and fitness through personalized training and supportive guidance.

Organized and hosted over 120 fitness events, including community 5Ks, wellness workshops, and transformation challenges, building a strong and connected fitness family.

Launched a virtual fitness platform, allowing members to train with us from anywhere, providing flexibility and convenience in achieving fitness goals.
